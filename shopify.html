Security Notice: Harmful Scripts Detected on Your Store

We have identified scripts currently running on your store that may pose a security or compliance risk.

This page explains what this means, why it matters, and the next steps required to protect your store.

What Are Custom Code Scripts?

Custom code scripts are pieces of code (commonly JavaScript) that run on your storefront to enable additional functionality.

These scripts may originate from:

  • Third-party applications
  • Tracking and analytics tools
  • Theme customizations
  • External integrations

Scripts operate within your store environment and can interact with customer activity, storefront behavior, and transaction processes.

Why This Notification Was Sent

Our systems detected script behavior that may:

  • Inject malicious code
  • Steal customer data
  • Redirect users without their knowledge or consent

In some cases, these risks may occur unintentionally due to misconfiguration, outdated integrations, or conflicts between multiple scripts.

Potential Impact

If these issues are not addressed, your store may experience:

Mild Impact

  • Slow store performance
  • Lower sales
  • Broken user experience (UX)

Moderate Impact

  • Checkout issues
  • Payment failures
  • App conflicts

Severe Impact

  • Account review
  • Payment holds (including via Shopify Payments)
  • Store suspension or termination

Important Considerations

Scripts can affect critical parts of your store, including:

  • Customer sessions
  • Cart and checkout behavior
  • Payment processing
  • Data handling

Because of this, identifying and resolving script-related risks requires careful handling. Incorrect changes may unintentionally disrupt store functionality or introduce additional issues.

Required Action

At this time, no manual changes are recommended.

To ensure your store remains secure and operational, please contact Shopify Support. Our team will:

  • Review the detected scripts
  • Help identify the source of the issue
  • Guide safe resolution without disrupting your store

Additional Information

Failure to address these risks may result in further review or enforcement actions in accordance with Shopify’s Terms of Service.

Maintaining a secure and compliant store is essential to protecting your customers and ensuring uninterrupted access to the platform.
